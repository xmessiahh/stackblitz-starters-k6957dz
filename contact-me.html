Garment District

The Garment District, located in Cambridge near Boston, is a beloved destination for vintage and secondhand clothing enthusiasts.
Known for its iconic "Dollar-A-Pound" section, shoppers can dig through piles of clothing and find unique treasures at an unbeatable price.
The store also offers a curated selection of vintage and contemporary pieces!
Ranging from retro dresses and funky jackets to stylish accessories.
With over 12,000 square feet of shopping space, it caters to all fashion tastes and budgets!
