Viviant Vintage

Vivant Vintage is a unique shop located in Allston, Massachusetts, known for its curated collection of vintage clothing, antiques, and accessories.
Viviant Vintage has been a staple of outer Boston by providing vintage clothing and sterling silver jewelry for over 20 years!
They have vintage including shirts, jackets, crewnecks, sportswear, western clothing, and famously their denim.
They have a cozy vibe and allow for the people of boston and all around to come and indudgle in the more higher caliber vinatge then your local savers.

Pros and Cons!

  1. Great selection of jeans from Levi's to Pepe to Akademiks!
  2. Racks on racks of shirts, hoodies, and jackets!
  3. Beautifuly made wall of a variety of sports wear from all eras!
  4. Very Pricey
  5. Always full of people
  6. Only two fitting rooms which causes a long waiting line
